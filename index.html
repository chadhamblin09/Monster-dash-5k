What is the Monster Dash 5K?

Join us for a spooktacular event where ghouls, goblins, and superheroes run for a cause! Dress in your best costume and run (or walk) 5K to help raise funds and awareness for Childhood Cancer through Primary Children’s Hospital.

👻 Highlights:

  • 🧡 Orange and 💜 Purple Halloween theme
  • 🧙 Costume contest with prizes
  • 🎶 Music, food trucks, and vendors
  • 🎁 Swag bag & T-shirt for participants
  • 🏅 Medals for top finishers in age groups
  • 🎗️ All funds go to Childhood Cancer Awareness
